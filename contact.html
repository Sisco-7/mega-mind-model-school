Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about contacting Mega Mind Model School.

How can I schedule a school tour?

Contact our admissions office via phone or email to book a tour. We offer tours on weekdays by appointment.

What are your office hours?

Our office is open Monday to Friday, 8:00 AM to 4:00 PM, and Saturday, 9:00 AM to 12:00 PM by appointment. We’re closed on Sundays and public holidays.

What is the best way to reach the admissions team?

Email us at info@megamindschool.com or use the contact form above for the fastest response.

Can I contact specific departments directly?

Yes, you can reach our admissions, academic, or administrative teams via email. Please specify your inquiry type in the contact form.

How do I get updates on school events?

Follow us on social media or subscribe to our newsletter via the contact form for the latest news and events.
