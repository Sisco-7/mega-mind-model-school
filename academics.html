Curriculum Highlights

Our curriculum blends Nigerian and international standards, focusing on:

  • STEM Education: Hands-on projects in science, technology, engineering, and math.
  • Arts & Culture: Music, drama, and visual arts to nurture creativity.
  • Character Development: Programs fostering integrity, leadership, and empathy.
  • Technology Integration: Digital literacy and coding for a tech-driven world.
