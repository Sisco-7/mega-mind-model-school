Welcome to Our Admission Process

At Mega Mind Model School, we are committed to making the admission process seamless and transparent. Our goal is to welcome students who are eager to learn and grow in a nurturing environment. Below, you’ll find detailed information about our admission process, requirements, tuition, and financial aid options.

Admission Process

Step 1: Inquiry

Contact our admissions team via our contact page or call us to learn more about our programs and schedule a tour.

Step 2: Application

Complete the application form available which will be sent to you by our support team. Ensure all required documents are submitted.

Step 3: Assessment

Applicants will undergo an age-appropriate assessment to evaluate their readiness for our programs.

Step 4: Interview

A brief interview with the student and parents/guardians to discuss goals and expectations.

Step 5: Admission Decision

Receive an admission decision within two weeks of completing all steps. Accepted students will receive an enrollment package.

Admission Requirements

We strive to ensure a smooth admission process. Below are the general requirements for each program:

  • Creche & Nursery (Ages 1–5): Birth certificate, immunization records, and a recent passport-sized photograph.
  • Primary (Ages 6–11): Birth certificate, previous school records (if applicable), immunization records, and a recent passport-sized photograph.
  • Secondary (Ages 12–17): Birth certificate, previous school records, standardized test results (if applicable), immunization records, and a recent passport-sized photograph.
  • Additional Requirements: A completed application form and a non-refundable application fee (contact us for details).

Note: Additional documents may be required based on the program or individual circumstances.

Tuition & Fees

Our tuition structure is designed to reflect the value of our comprehensive programs while remaining accessible. For detailed tuition information, please contact our admissions office.

  • Creche & Nursery: Includes meals, learning materials, and basic extracurricular activities.
  • Primary: Covers core academic programs, access to digital tools, and select extracurricular activities.
  • Secondary: Includes advanced academic programs, STEM resources, and career counseling services.
  • Additional Fees: Uniforms, transportation, and optional extracurricular programs may incur additional costs.

Financial Aid & Scholarships

We believe in making quality education accessible. Mega Mind Model School offers financial aid and scholarship opportunities based on merit and need.

  • Merit-Based Scholarships: Available for students with exceptional academic or extracurricular achievements.
  • Need-Based Financial Aid: Support for families demonstrating financial need.
  • Sibling Discounts: Reduced tuition for families with multiple enrolled students.

To apply for financial aid or scholarships, include the relevant application form with your admission documents.
