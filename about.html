About Mega Mind Model School

Building a Legacy of Excellence Since 2009

Our History

Established in 2009 by Mrs. A.O. Adedeji, Mega Mind Model School began with a vision to provide exceptional education in Lagos, Nigeria. From a modest start, we have grown into a leading institution, serving over a thousand students with a commitment to academic rigor, character development, and community engagement.

Our Philosophy

At Mega Mind, we believe education goes beyond academics. Our philosophy centers on holistic development, fostering critical thinking, creativity, and ethical values to prepare students for a dynamic world.

Integrity

We foster honesty and ethical behavior, guiding students to become principled leaders.

Excellence

We strive for outstanding academic and personal achievements in all endeavors.

Innovation

Community

Located in the heart of Lagos, our campus features modern classrooms, science and computer labs, a library, and sports facilities, creating an inspiring environment for learning and growth.
